Hobbies

I love reading books! Mainly, I like to read fantasy and fiction books (Brandon Sanderson, Tolkien), but I am also open to any kind of books really! One of my favorite books is The Little Prince by Antoine de Saint-Exupery as well as Mistborn by Sanderson. My most recently read bood is The Stranger by Albert Camus. As you can see, I love reading about a whole lot of different topics.

My favorite sport is tennis. Although I am by no means good, I really enjoy playing with friends. I started playing when I was 12 years old as a recreational sport and never really got into the competitive side of it. In college, I hope I can reconnect with my love for tennis and find other people with this same interest.

I am a complete nerd when it comes to board games. I am always willing to sit and hear someone explain the rules to their favorite game.
